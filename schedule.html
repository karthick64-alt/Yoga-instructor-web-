How to choose your class

New to yoga? Start with Morning Grounding or Sunday Reset for slower pacing and extra explanation. Comfortable with standing poses and basic transitions? Try Alignment Essentials or Strength & Stability.

Levels

  • Gentle: Slow, supported, great for beginners.
  • All Levels: Options given to scale up or down.
  • Intermediate: For students familiar with basic poses and healthy to bear weight on wrists.

Need help deciding? Send me a quick note with your experience and any injuries.

Class Descriptions

Find the practice that resonates with where you are today

Gentle

Morning Grounding

Tue & Thu · 7:00 – 8:00 AM

Start your day with intention. This gentle Hatha flow focuses on waking up the spine, opening the breath, and setting a calm tone for the hours ahead. Perfect for beginners or anyone seeking a slower, more meditative practice.

  • Slow, mindful movements
  • Breath awareness
  • Gentle stretches
  • Props encouraged
All Levels

Alignment Essentials

Mon & Wed · 6:00 – 7:15 PM

Build a strong foundation with Iyengar-inspired precision. We explore fundamental poses with detailed alignment cues, using props to support and deepen your understanding. Options are given for every body.

  • Detailed alignment instruction
  • Prop-based learning
  • Standing & seated poses
  • Modifications for all levels
Intermediate

Strength & Stability

Fri · 6:30 – 7:45 PM

A dynamic Vinyasa flow that builds strength, balance, and confidence. We move through sequences that challenge your body while maintaining mindful alignment. Best for those comfortable with basic poses.

  • Dynamic sequences
  • Strength-building poses
  • Balance work
  • Intermediate variations
Restorative

Sunday Reset

Sun · 5:00 – 6:15 PM

End your week with deep rest. This restorative practice uses props, longer holds, and gentle movements to calm the nervous system. Ideal for stress relief, recovery, and anyone needing gentle movement.

  • Fully supported poses
  • Nervous system regulation
  • Extended relaxation
  • Perfect for recovery
