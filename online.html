Getting Started Online

Everything you need to know for a smooth virtual practice

What You'll Need

  • Stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps)
  • Device with camera (laptop, tablet, or phone)
  • Yoga mat (or non-slip surface)
  • Zoom app (free download)
  • Optional: blocks, strap, blanket, bolster

Setting Up Your Space

  • Find a quiet area with enough room to move
  • Position your device so I can see your full mat
  • Good lighting helps me see your alignment
  • Have water nearby
  • Wear comfortable clothes you can move in

Before Class

  • You'll receive a Zoom link 24 hours before class
  • Test your connection 10 minutes early
  • Mute your microphone when you join
  • Turn on your camera if comfortable
  • Have props or alternatives ready

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have my camera on?

While it's helpful for me to see your alignment and offer corrections, having your camera on is optional. Your comfort and privacy are important.

What if I don't have yoga props?

No problem! I'll guide you on using household items‚Äîbooks for blocks, belts for straps, pillows for bolsters. You can practice effectively with what you have.

Can beginners join online classes?

Absolutely! Online classes are perfect for beginners. I provide clear verbal cues and visual demonstrations. You can also pause or rewind recordings if needed.

How do I access class recordings?

After each live class, you'll receive an email with a secure link to the recording. It's available for 7 days so you can practice at your own pace.

What if I have technical issues during class?

I'm here to help! Reach out via email before class if you need technical support. During class, use the chat feature and I'll assist as quickly as possible.

Can I switch between online and in-studio classes?

Yes! Your class packages work for both formats. Simply book the format that works best for you each week.
